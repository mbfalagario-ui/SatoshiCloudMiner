Satoshi Cloud Miner 1.0.0 — Build #14 (Operator unlimited withdrawal)

What's new in this build:

NEW — Operator unlimited withdrawal
- Admin operators now see a dedicated "OPERATOR — withdraw any amount · 0%
  fee" badge on the Wallet tab and can drain any balance amount to a
  Lightning invoice/address at 0% commission with no per-transaction
  minimum. All other users continue to operate under the standard
  150,000 sats minimum + 10% flat network fee.
- The backend treats this as a privileged path tied to is_admin=true and
  records the action in the operator audit log just like every other
  admin action.
