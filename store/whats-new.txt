Satoshi Cloud Miner 1.0.0 — Build #12 (TestFlight stability + UX polish)

What's fixed in this build:
- Eliminated a rare native crash that could occur when signing out of the
  app. Sign-out now completes cleanly and routes back to the welcome
  screen without interrupting the app session.
- The Home dashboard now refreshes its hashrate, active miner count, and
  daily projection automatically whenever you return to it from the Mine,
  Wallet, or Profile tabs — purchases are reflected immediately, no app
  restart required.
- After buying a mining plan you are now automatically returned to the
  Home dashboard so you can see your new miners in action.
- Improved purchase confirmation text: ad-removal purchases now show a
  dedicated "Ad-Free Unlocked" message instead of the generic "0 miner
  added" wording from earlier builds.
- Defensive fallback for transactions Apple has already redeemed: the app
  now surfaces a clear "Purchase recorded — pull to refresh" hint instead
  of an empty success toast.

Previous changes still in place:
- Real Apple In-App Purchase via StoreKit 2 for all 10 mining plans and
  the Ad-Free $3.99 tier.
- Lightning withdrawals (BOLT11 + Lightning Address) with 150,000 sats
  minimum, 10% flat fee.
- Server-side daily check-in cron and optional auto-reinvest.
- Operator (admin) console with revenue analytics, user moderation, and
  transaction inspector.
- AI Trading Agents simulation with daily P&L + win-rate per strategy.
- AI market commentary ticker refreshed daily.
