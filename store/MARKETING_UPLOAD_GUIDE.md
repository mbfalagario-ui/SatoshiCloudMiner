# App Store Connect — Build #16 Marketing Upload Guide

> All assets generated by AI (Gemini Nano Banana) and stored in
> `/app/store/marketing/`. Upload via the App Store Connect web UI
> (https://appstoreconnect.apple.com).

## 1) App Store Icon
- **File**: `/app/store/marketing/icon-1024.png` (1024×1024, PNG, no transparency)
- **Where**: App Store Connect → My Apps → Satoshi Cloud Miner →
  iOS App → 1.0 Prepare for Submission → App Information → App Icon
- **Note**: This is already baked into the iOS binary as the home-screen icon
  (`/app/frontend/assets/images/icon.png`) and shipped with Build #16. The
  PNG above is the same image at 1024×1024 for the App Store listing slot.

## 2) Promotional Banners
The five 16:9 banners in `/app/store/marketing/` are NOT directly uploaded
to App Store Connect — Apple uses a fixed set of phone-screenshot slots,
not free-form banners. However, they are bundled into the App Preview
video below. You can also use them on:
- Your marketing site
- Social media (Twitter/X cover, Instagram posts)
- Press releases

Generated assets:
- `banner-hero.png`            – Hero / "Featured" banner
- `banner-feature-mine.png`    – AI-Optimized Mining Plans feature slide
- `banner-feature-yield.png`   – Instant Lightning Withdrawals feature slide
- `banner-feature-support.png` – Premium Support feature slide
- `banner-cta.png`             – Closing "Download free on the App Store" slide

## 3) App Preview Video
- **File**: `/app/store/marketing/app-preview-15-30s.mp4`
  (1080×1920 portrait, H.264, 28.03 seconds, silent, ~0.6 MB)
- **Where**: App Store Connect → 1.0 Prepare for Submission →
  iPhone 6.7" Display → **App Previews** slot → Choose File → upload
- **Note**: Apple allows ONE preview per device size class. We use the
  6.7" slot (iPhone 16 Pro Max class). Apple lets it cascade to smaller
  device classes if you don't upload separate previews.

## Apple ad-content review tips
- We intentionally do **NOT** state earnings figures anywhere in marketing
  or in-app screenshots (Apple guideline 4.3.0 + FTC). Don't add any
  "$X/day" or "X% APY" claims to the App Store page.
- We say "Bitcoin mining performance monitor" not "Bitcoin earnings app".
- IAP descriptions in App Store Connect must match the in-app prices.
