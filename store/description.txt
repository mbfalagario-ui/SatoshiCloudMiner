Satoshi Cloud Miner — Bitcoin mining performance dashboard.

Monitor your simulated cloud-mining cluster, track Lightning payouts, and
watch AI Trading Agents optimise yield in real time. Built for hobbyists
and operators who want a clean, dark, neon-accented control panel for
their Bitcoin-side activity.

WHAT'S INSIDE
- Live cluster dashboard: hashrate, daily projection, lifetime earnings
- AI Trading Agents card with daily P&L and win-rate per strategy
- Daily AI market commentary ticker
- Cloud mining plans across 10 tiers (starter to flagship farm)
- Lightning-only withdrawals to any BOLT11 invoice or Lightning address
- Auto daily check-in (server-side) and optional auto-reinvest
- Referral program with shareable code

FOR OPERATORS
- Admin operator console: revenue, paid-out, profit margin, fees collected
- Per-user moderation: ban/unban, grant admin, credit/debit balance
- Transaction inspector with status overrides and audit log

Outcomes depend on server status and operational conditions. Pricing
